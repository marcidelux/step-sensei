# Our Mission
<span style={{ fontSize: '1.4rem' }}>
\
LogicInfo is a playful logic and strategy program that combines board games, mathematics, and digital creativity.
Its goal is to develop concentration, problem-solving skills, and visual thinking.
<br />
The main elements of our program:


- Chess, checkers, other board games — developing strategic thinking and logic for both children and adults


- Playful, thought-provoking puzzles — practicing creative problem-solving


- Introduction to programming and algorithmic thinking — developing digital thinking and creativity
